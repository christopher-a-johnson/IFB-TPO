Save a custom "Pipeline view" to control the loans that display on your Pipeline. A Pipeline view can consist of the Pipeline columns to display, the sort order of the column data, a search criteria, and the All Loans or My Loans indicator.
After managing the columns, sorting the data, applying search criteria, or selecting All Loans or My Loans (where applicable), save the new configuration as a new custom view.
Once you save a view, you can select it at any time from the Pipeline View list to apply the criteria to your Pipeline.
